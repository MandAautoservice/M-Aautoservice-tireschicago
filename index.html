Driver Intelligence Hub

Essential safety tools and seasonal guides for Niles & Chicagoland drivers.

Is My Tire Safe?

Combine DOT age, tread depth, and pressure into one quick check.

The Penny Test: Insert a penny head-first into the tread. If you can see all of Lincoln's head, your tread is below 2/32" and legally unsafe.

TPMS Warning Light?

Don't ignore the horseshoe light. Here is exactly what it means:

Solid Light: Low pressure. Usually triggered by sudden Chicago temperature drops. Fill to the PSI listed inside your driver's door.
Flashing Light: Sensor malfunction or dead battery. Requires diagnostic scan.

Chicago Winter Guide

Niles local regulations and the 45°F (7°C) safety threshold.

  • The 45°F Rule: Summer and all-season rubber hardens below 45°F, drastically increasing stopping distance.
  • Salt Damage: Cook County road salt accelerates sidewall cracking. Wash wheels weekly in winter.
